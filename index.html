Video of HILARY HAHN

HILARY HAHN
Hilary Hahn was born November 27, 1979. She is an American violinist. She has performed throughout the world both as a soloist with leading orchestras and conductors and as a recitalist. She has also built a reputation as a champion of contemporary music. She began playing the violin one month before her fourth birthday. She participated in a Suzuki class for a year. From 1985 to 1990 she studied in Baltimore. In 1990, at ten, Hahn was admitted to the Curtis Institute of Music in Philadelphia. She studied with Brodsky for seven years and learned the études of Kreutzer, Ševčík, Gaviniès, and Rode, and the Paganini Caprices. She also learned 28 violin concertos, chamber works, and assorted showpieces.
